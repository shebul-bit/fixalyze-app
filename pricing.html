Frequently asked questions

Do I need a Shopify account to use Fixalyze? +
No. You just paste your store URL and Fixalyze audits the publicly visible storefront. No Shopify login, no API access, no sensitive credentials needed.
How is this different from Google Lighthouse or Hotjar? +
Google Lighthouse measures page speed and accessibility. Hotjar shows you raw heatmap data. Neither tells you what to fix or why it's costing you sales. Fixalyze gives you a prioritised list of specific UX and conversion issues with exact fix instructions — written for Shopify store owners who don't have a developer.
What does the AI actually analyse? +
Fixalyze scrapes your live store and analyses it against established UX research and ecommerce conversion best practices. It checks for missing trust signals, checkout friction, mobile UX issues, product page weaknesses, navigation problems, social proof gaps, and more — then scores your store and prioritises what to fix first.
When are paid plans launching? +
Very soon. Run a free audit and join the waitlist — you'll be first to know and receive an exclusive early-bird discount when we launch. Free tier is available right now with no signup required.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. No contracts, no minimum terms. Cancel whenever you like. Monthly subscribers can cancel before their next billing date. Yearly subscribers are billed once upfront.
Does it work for non-Shopify stores? +
Fixalyze is optimised specifically for Shopify — the fix recommendations are tailored to what's achievable in Shopify's theme editor without a developer. It will audit other ecommerce sites but the Shopify-specific guidance won't apply.
